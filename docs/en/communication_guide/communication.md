# Communication Guide

We welcome contributions to the msMemScope community. If you have any questions or suggestions, contact us in the following ways:

## 1. Issues

Submitting [Issues](https://gitcode.com/Ascend/msmemscope/issues) in the GitCode repository is the most direct way to provide your feedback. Before submitting an issue, refer to the [Issue Submission and Handling Guide](./how_to_issue.md) to submit an issue that complies with the specifications to improve our processing efficiency.

## 2. Email

If your issue is complicated or you want to keep it confidential, send an email to:

**<memscope@outlook.com>**

Describe your question or suggestion in detail in the email. We will reply as soon as possible.

## 3. Open-source assistant

Scan the QR code to add the Ascend open-source assistant, obtain the group link, and join the MindStudio community technical exchange group to obtain help and support.
